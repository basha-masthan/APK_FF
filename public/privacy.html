WinZone Arena ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and personal information in accordance with applicable data protection laws, including the Information Technology Act of India and the principles of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) as applicable to Indian users. Please read this Privacy Policy to understand how we collect, use, protect, and share your information when you use our gaming app and platform.

By downloading, registering or using our app, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this Privacy Policy. If you disagree with any part of this policy, please discontinue use of our app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email, mobile number, username, date of birth, profile photo.
  • Account Credentials: Securely stored password (encrypted), login device details.
  • Gameplay Data: Tournament and match participation, scores, in-game IDs, performance and achievement data.
  • Transaction Data: Wallet balance, deposit and withdrawal history, payment method (no sensitive card data is stored by us).
  • Usage Information: Device info, IP address, time zone, clickstream, session and engagement data, crash/error reports.
  • Communications: Support requests, app feedback, messages.
  • Location Data: Used only to verify country eligibility, not stored or shared.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To register and manage your account and gameplay.
  • To process transactions and send prize payments securely.
  • To improve app features, personalize your experience, and provide support.
  • To prevent fraud, enforce terms, and ensure fair play.
  • To send you important notifications, updates, offers (you can opt out anytime).
  • To comply with legal/regulatory requirements and cooperate with government agencies where required by law.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

  • We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party for their marketing purposes.
  • Information may be shared:
    • With trusted partners (e.g. payment services) to process payments securely
    • For analytics and app improvement, with trusted service providers. Only anonymized, non-personalized data is shared for analytics.
    • With authorities if required by law, legal process, or to protect our rights and users

4. Data Security

  • All personal data is encrypted and stored securely on trusted servers.
  • We use SSL technology for all data transfers, best practices for storage, and strong access controls.
  • Regular security checks and audits to detect and prevent unauthorized access or data leaks.
However, no system is 100% secure. In case of a breach, affected users will be notified within 72 hours and efforts will be made to minimize any potential harm.

5. User Rights and Choices

  • Contact us for questions or complaints regarding privacy.

6. Data Retention

  • Your data is kept only as long as necessary for the app’s functions, legal compliance, and resolution of any disputes.
  • After account deletion, your information is deleted or anonymized within 30 days unless retention is required by law (e.g. financial records).

If we learn a child’s personal data has been provided, we will delete it promptly. If you are a parent/guardian and believe your child used our app without consent, please contact us.
